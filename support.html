Support & Help

ʻĀina Atlas — Every parcel in Hawaiʻi, mapped with the kaona of its name.

Getting Started

  • Open the app and zoom in on any Hawaiian island.
  • Orange parcel boundaries appear automatically when you zoom in close.
  • Tap any parcel to see its boundaries, ahupuaʻa, Hawaiian name context, and hazard data.
  • Use the search bar to find a specific address or parcel tax map key (TMK).

Account & Sign In

  • ʻĀina Atlas uses Sign In with Apple — no separate password needed.
  • Tap the Profile tab and choose "Sign in with Apple" to create or sign in to your account.
  • To delete your account: go to Profile → scroll down → tap "Delete Account".

Subscriptions & Purchases

  • Browsing parcel boundaries and basic info is free.
  • Mālama Property Brief ($4.99/mo) unlocks AI-generated cultural context, hazard summaries, and land history for any parcel.
  • Atlas Pro ($19.99/mo) adds unlimited reports, advanced hazard overlays, and bulk export.
  • To manage or cancel: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ʻĀina Atlas.
  • Parcel Reports are one-time purchases per parcel. Once purchased, they are available in the Reports tab.

Data & Privacy

  • Parcel boundary data is sourced from Hawaiʻi state GIS records.
  • ʻĀina Atlas does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for full details.

Troubleshooting

  • Parcels not loading: Zoom in closer — boundaries only appear past a certain zoom level to keep the map fast.
  • Subscription not unlocking: Tap Profile → Restore Purchases.
  • App crashing: Force-quit and reopen. If it persists, delete and reinstall.
